My Process

1. Client Brief

Meet with the client to understand their requirements, goals, and expectations for the project. Gather all necessary information.

2. Research and Inspiration

Conduct research, gather references, and explore design trends to gain inspiration and insights for the project.

3. Concept Development

Brainstorm ideas and concepts based on the brief and research. Sketch out rough drafts and create initial design concepts.

4. Design Iterations

Create multiple design iterations, refining and improving the chosen concepts. Seek feedback from the client and make necessary revisions.

5. Finalization and Delivery

Present the final design to the client, addressing any final feedback. Prepare and deliver the necessary files and assets for the project.
